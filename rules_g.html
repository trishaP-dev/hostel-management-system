RULES AND GUIDELINES

1. General Conduct
Students must maintain discipline and follow hostel rules at all times. Respect hostel staff, wardens, and fellow residents. Any form of ragging, bullying, or harassment is strictly prohibited. Maintain cleanliness in rooms, corridors, and common areas.
2. Timings & Attendance
Hostel gates close at 10:00 PM (or as per the instituteâ€™s rules). Late entries will require prior permission. Attendance is mandatory at roll-call (if applicable). Prior approval is required for staying out overnight or leaving the hostel premises for vacations.
3. Room & Property Care
Keep rooms neat and tidy. Regular inspections will be conducted. Do not damage hostel property (walls, furniture, electrical appliances, etc.). Report any maintenance issues immediately to hostel authorities. No alteration or shifting of furniture is allowed.
4. Visitors & Guests
Visitors are only allowed in designated areas during visiting hours. No outsiders or guests are allowed inside hostel rooms. Any violation will lead to strict disciplinary action.
5. Noise & Disturbances
Maintain silence after 10:00 PM to ensure a peaceful study environment. Loud music, shouting, or any disturbances are not permitted.
6. Electrical & Fire Safety
Unauthorized electrical appliances (heaters, irons, kettles, etc.) are not allowed. Cooking inside the rooms is strictly prohibited. Do not tamper with electrical fittings or fire safety equipment.
7. Food & Mess Regulations
Mess food should not be taken to rooms unless permission is granted for medical reasons. Maintain proper queue and decorum in the mess. Wastage of food should be avoided.
8. Prohibited Activities
Strictly no smoking, alcohol, drugs, or any illegal substances inside the hostel. Gambling or possession of weapons is strictly prohibited. Any involvement in fights, theft, or misconduct will result in strict action.
9. Internet & Wi-Fi Usage
Internet usage should be for educational purposes only. Accessing inappropriate or restricted websites is strictly prohibited.
10. Medical & Emergency
Report any medical issues to the warden immediately. In case of emergency, contact the hostel authorities or designated emergency numbers.
11. Leave & Vacating Rules
Students must seek permission before leaving the hostel for vacations. Before vacating the hostel, all dues must be cleared, and rooms should be returned in proper condition. Disciplinary Actions Violation of any of the above rules will result in strict action, including fines, suspension, or expulsion from the hostel.
